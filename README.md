How to Build Characters in Endfield
Choose one core carry first
An S Tier or A Tier operator is usually your best early investment because they deliver strong overall value across difficult content.
Upgrade what improves consistency
Most progression slows down because of downtime, survivability failures, or role-mismatched equipment. Builds should reduce these problems first.
Optimize after the basics are stable
After you can clear consistently, you can push for faster clears and higher damage ceilings.
